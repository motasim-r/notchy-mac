# Notchy iOS Brand Guidelines

## Direction
- Tone: premium, calm, camera-first.
- Surface style: dark glass with restrained contrast.
- Objective: keep attention on eyes/camera while script stays near notch area.

## Typography
- Display headings: `Season Serif`.
- UI/body/controls: `Season Sans`.
- Numeric telemetry: system monospaced.

Bundled files:
- `SeasonSans-TRIAL-Regular.otf`
- `SeasonSans-TRIAL-Medium.otf`
- `SeasonSans-TRIAL-Bold.otf`
- `SeasonSerif-TRIAL-Regular.otf`
- `SeasonSerif-TRIAL-Medium.otf`
- `SeasonSerif-TRIAL-Bold.otf`

Font note:
- Current files are `TRIAL` fonts. Confirm commercial licensing before broad production distribution.

## Color + Surfaces
- Background: deep graphite near-black.
- Overlay body: black, high-opacity.
- Cards/trays: black with low alpha and subtle white border.
- Primary text: near-white.
- Secondary text: white alpha 55-80%.

## Shape
- Notch overlay silhouette should remain notch-inspired with shoulder carve.
- Rounded corners should be smooth and consistent with bottom tray + sheet cards.

## Motion
- Keep transitions short and smooth.
- Avoid flashy animation; prioritize readability and low distraction.

## Layout Rules
- Full-screen camera is primary context.
- Teleprompter remains top-center.
- Bottom tray holds active controls.
- Detailed controls stay in bottom sheet tabs.
